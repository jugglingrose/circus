School Assemblies & Summer Library Programs

Corean offers unique programs specifically for school assemblies and summer library programs!

School Assembly Show

This high-energy, interactive hula hoop program promotes perseverance, confidence, positive encouragement, creativity and movement. Students don’t just watch! They participate, move, and build confidence.



Educational & Developmental Benefits

  • Builds confidence through participation
  • Encourages teamwork and respectful peer encouragement
  • Inspires creativity
  • Promotes joyful, healthy movement
  • Creates shared positive experiences


What Students Experience

  • Exciting professional hula hoop performance
  • Fun audience participation, interactive hoop games, and team challenges.
  • Encouragement to try new things


Program Details

  • 20-50 minute program customized to your schedule
  • Ideal for ages 6 - 12.


Simple Setup

  • Cafeteria, gymnasium, or auditorium
  • 15 X 15 ft minimum performance space
