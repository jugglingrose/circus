ABOUT

Welcome, thank you for visiting! My name is Corean and I am a cirque artist & variety artist. I am most known for my unique hula hooping acts involving up to 7 hula hoops or over 40 on my waist. In addition, I have skills in juggling, dancing, LED performance and more. My skill sets have allowed me to create some unique variety shows.


My performances are so much more than just standing on a stage and doing tricks. My pieces have character and typically combine dance, theatre, and/or storytelling elements while offering a fun & thrilling experience to the audience. They come complete with beautiful costuming and are meticulously choreographed to my own music. Plus, my studies in theatre/clowning/improv have taught me how to engage and connect with the audience while performing on stage.
